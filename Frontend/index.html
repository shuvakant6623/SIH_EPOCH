Key Features

Comprehensive tools and technologies designed to enhance electrical safety through community participation and AI-powered detection.

Crowdsourced Reporting

Submit geotagged reports with photos and videos of LT line breaks directly from your mobile device with GPS precision and offline capabilities.

AI Detection System

AI-powered authenticity analysis of reports to understand ground realities and identify genuine electrical hazards with high accuracy.

Real-time Dashboard

Interactive map visualization with dynamic hotspots, live data streams, and comprehensive analytics for KSEBL operators and field teams.

Vernacular Reporting

Vernacular reporting enables community members to submit line break information in Malayalam or English via voice or text, ensuring inclusive participation.

Instant Alerts

Real-time alerts to KSEBL control room and field operations teams with exact location coordinates and severity assessment for immediate response.

Offline Reporting & Sync

Allows citizens to submit reports without internet; data is stored locally and synced automatically when connectivity is restored during power outages.

About EPOCH

Emergency Power Outage Control Hub - Revolutionizing electrical safety through community-driven intelligence and AI-powered detection technology. Kerala's extensive electrical grid serves over 35 million citizens across 14 districts, facing challenges from LT line breaks, transformer failures, and electrical hazards during monsoons and extreme weather.

Our Mission

EPOCH empowers Kerala's communities to actively participate in electrical safety through crowdsourced reporting and real-time AI detection. We bridge the gap between citizen observations and KSEBL response systems to create a comprehensive hazard monitoring network.

Our Vision

To establish Kerala as a leader in community-driven electrical hazard management, where every citizen becomes a guardian of power grid safety through technology-enabled reporting and collaborative intelligence systems.

How It Works

EPOCH combines geotagged citizen reports with AI-powered authenticity analysis to create real-time hazard maps. Our platform processes multilingual content, validates reports through AI verification, and provides predictive analytics to forecast potential danger zones.
